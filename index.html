I’ve been an artist my entire life, but my music journey began in 2017, shortly after losing a close friend to suicide. At the time, I had no idea how much music was going to influence my life. I’ve made so many great friends, connections, earned a decent living, and had opportunities I never thought possible.

Until 2024, I worked under the alias “MMXVII” but I realized the style I become known for wasn’t true to me. So I switched entirely to electronic music, focusing on retro game inspired sounds mixed with hard bass, and sometimes vocals by myself or other artists.
